David Benbennick wrote this file, based on the information at Flags of the World. The colors are those given at Vexilla Mundi: rgb(206,17,38) for red, rgb(0,114,198) for blue, and rgb(252,209,22) for yellow.

The Flag Creator: The flag dates from the achievement of self-government in 1967. It was the winning design in a competition that more than 600 local people entered. The designer and winner was Sir Reginald Samuel.
